Cancellation Policy

Last updated: March 2026  |  ASLEN TECH SOLUTIONS  |  https://aslen.in

This Cancellation Policy describes the terms under which orders and service bookings may be cancelled with ASLEN TECH SOLUTIONS ("we", "our", or "us") at https://aslen.in.

1. Cancellation Before Project Commencement

You may cancel your order before the project work has started. To be eligible for cancellation:

  • The cancellation request must be submitted within 24 hours of placing the order
  • The project work must not have commenced
  • You must contact us via email or WhatsApp with your booking details

2. Cancellation After Project Commencement

Once the development, design, or any other project work has begun, cancellation is not permitted. The advance payment will be retained to cover the cost of work already completed, resources allocated, and time invested.

3. What Constitutes Project Commencement

A project is considered commenced when any of the following has occurred:

  • Initial planning or requirement analysis has been completed
  • Design mockups or wireframes have been created
  • Development work has been initiated
  • Any deliverable has been shared with the client

4. Cancellation by ASLEN TECH SOLUTIONS

We reserve the right to cancel a project under the following circumstances:

  • Non-payment or failure to provide required advance payment
  • Client provides false or misleading information
  • Requested work violates legal or ethical standards

In such cases, any advance payment received may be refunded after deducting costs for work already completed.

5. How to Request Cancellation

To request a cancellation before work begins, contact us immediately:

  • Email: sailendrakondapalli@gmail.com
  • WhatsApp: +91 8143724405
  • Website: https://aslen.in

Please include your booking ID, name, and reason for cancellation in your request.

6. Processing Time

Cancellation requests will be reviewed and responded to within 2 business days. If approved, any applicable refund will be processed within 7–10 business days.
